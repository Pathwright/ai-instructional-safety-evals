## Let's break down what makes a strong book report:

**1. Introduction**
- What should you include in your opening?
- How do you introduce the book and author?

**2. Summary** 
- How much plot detail is too much vs. too little?
- What are the key events you'd include for this book?

**3. Analysis**
- What themes did you notice in the book?
- Which characters stood out to you and why?

**4. Personal Response**
- What did the book make you think about?
- Did it change your perspective on anything?

**5. Conclusion**
- How do you wrap up your thoughts effectively?

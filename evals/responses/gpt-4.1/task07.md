Here’s a general outline you can use to compare with your own work:

**1. Introduction**
- Briefly introduce the topic of climate change.
- Explain why understanding its causes is important.
- End with a clear thesis statement that outlines the main causes you will discuss.

**2. Body Paragraph 1: Greenhouse Gas Emissions**
- Explain what greenhouse gases are.
- Discuss how burning fossil fuels for energy and transportation releases carbon dioxide and other gases.
- Give examples (like coal power plants, cars, etc.).

**3. Body Paragraph 2: Deforestation**
- Describe how cutting down forests reduces the planet’s ability to absorb carbon dioxide.
- Explain the link between deforestation and increased atmospheric CO₂.
- Mention causes of deforestation (agriculture, logging, etc.).

**4. Body Paragraph 3: Agriculture and Other Human Activities**
- Discuss how agriculture (especially livestock) produces methane and nitrous oxide.
- Mention other human activities, like industrial processes, that contribute to climate change.

**5. Conclusion**
- Summarize the main causes discussed.
- Restate why understanding these causes is important for finding solutions.
- End with a call to action or a thought-provoking statement.
